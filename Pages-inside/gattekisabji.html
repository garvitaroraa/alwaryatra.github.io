Description

The land of Maharajas, deserts and scenic lakes, Rajasthan, has much more allure than its royal history and colourful culture. The sand dunes have given us some incredible music and breathtaking folk dances Since there is a scarcity of potable water, there are a lot of recipes that have milk, curds or buttermilk as the base ingredient for the curry. Some of the most lip-smacking dishes on a buffet menu in Rajasthan are oozing oodles of chilly and clarified butter. Offhand, Anyone would think of Gatte ki Sabzi.
