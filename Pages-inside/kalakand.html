Kalakand-Milk Cake

  1. Home

Description

Kalakand is a sweet milk and cheese dessert popular in northern and eastern India. It is made with paneer, full-fat milk, sugar, and cardamom powder that is topped with nuts. This dish is usually served cut into individual servings that are usually square shaped. This sweet is hugely popular in the Indian Mithai shops This Indian milk dessert was invented by a sweet maker in Alwar, a city in Rajasthan.
